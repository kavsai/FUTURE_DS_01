# 📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project was completed as part of the **Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics ** internship.

The objective was to analyze business sales data, identify performance trends, and build a professional dashboard to support business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Clean and analyze sales data
- Identify revenue trends over time
- Find top-selling products
- Analyze sales by category and region
- Evaluate profitability
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- WPS Spreadsheet
- Pivot Tables
- Charts & Graphs
- Business Analytics Techniques

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Region
- Profit by Category

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Sales trends vary over time, indicating seasonal demand.
- Top-selling products contribute significantly to total revenue.
- High-performing categories generate the majority of sales.
- Regional analysis highlights the strongest sales markets.
- Profit analysis helps identify the most profitable categories.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing on high-performing products.
- Increase inventory for top-selling products.
- Invest more in profitable categories.
- Improve performance in lower-performing regions.
- Monitor sales trends regularly for better planning.
